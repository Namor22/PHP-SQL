Information About me

Fan of video games, I started this passion on computer at age 5. By the time, online and multiplayer weren't as common as nowadays and I grew up enjoying the narrative modes of my beloved games.
I quickly get interest in the Arcade Racing Games to later move on to games centered on History and war (my 2nd passion). I had the chance to experience the very first opus of today's main franchises on the market, and watch them evolving along the years.

But sometimes life's taking you on a different path.

While pursuing an Engineer formation in apprenticeship (Master lvl), I spent 3 years in the automotive industry. There, among others, I was in charge on controls means and prepare the 3D scan of out products.

Then, I received a new challenge: taking the lead and managing the implantation of the 1st automatized workshop for a furniture company.
I always had the desire to discover other countries and challenge myself, I accepted and moved abroad 2 years in Romania for the job.

Finally in 2022, i integrated the gaming business school
